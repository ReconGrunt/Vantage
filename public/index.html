Ceiling alignment (fisheye)
Ceiling shape (paint mask)

Project onto your ceiling, hit Paint, then brush: Reveal sky carves the sky into your ceiling's true shape; Black out hides the spill onto walls/edges. Tip: Fill black then reveal just the ceiling. Saved automatically; hit Paint again to stop and look around.

Point the projector straight up at the ceiling and lie beneath it. Ceiling skylight is the correct projection for a flat roof — a round window of overhead sky, just as if you were looking up through it (the zenith sits at centre). Fisheye dome is only correct on a curved planetarium dome. Use “Visible sky” to widen the window and the compass to spin North to your room.
